About our Project

What make we choose this Project ?

"TRAO" is a fundraising campaign initiated by a group of FPT University students with the purpose of supporting shelters and creating an exchange playground between FPT students and children in shelters - to spread love to everyone with the surrounding community

With the slogan "Giving knowledge and sending love", we want to bring you the meaningful value of books and at the same time bring love to children in shelters.
